Co-Author

From Treatment to Prevention: Restructuring Biomedical Research and Regulation for an Aging World

by Arundhathi Jathin and Vynavin Vinod

Healthspan Action Coalition (HSAC)

A comprehensive policy paper examining the challenges of longevity science and aging biology research across North America. The paper analyzes current regulatory frameworks, including the FDA's Expanded Access program and state-level Right-to-Try laws, and proposes reforms to accelerate the translation of aging research into accessible therapies. Covers regional analyses of the West Coast, East Coast, Florida, Georgia, and Canada, with specific focus on healthspan extension and preventive medicine.

Key Contributions:

  • Analysis of biomedical research funding across multiple regions
  • Evaluation of patient access pathways to experimental therapies
  • Policy recommendations for longevity science advancement
  • Comparative study of regulatory frameworks in US and Canada
Published: September 10, 2025 Healthspan Action Coalition Policy, Longevity, Healthcare
